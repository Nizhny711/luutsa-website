About Dr. Luutsa Felix Edwine

Learn more about his journey, expertise, and commitment to transformative leadership.

Introduction

Dr. Luutsa Felix Edwine is a highly experienced academic, researcher, and consultant with over 15 years of dedicated work in public sector reforms, organizational systems, and governance. He is committed to driving strategic transformations in both public and private institutions, effectively combining deep theoretical expertise with practical, actionable insights. His passion lies in empowering organizations to achieve their full potential through robust systems and ethical leadership.

Philosophy

“Institutions thrive when systems work — and systems work when driven by purpose, leadership, and clarity.”

Academic Background

  • PhD in Public Administration – Moi University
  • MA in Public Administration – University of Nairobi
  • BA in Political Science and Public Administration – Moi University

Core Areas of Expertise

  • Organizational Systems & Structures
  • Strategic Management & Planning
  • Governance & Public Sector Reforms
  • Policy Development & Implementation
  • Research, Monitoring & Evaluation
  • Training, Mentorship, and Capacity Building

Professional Highlights

  • Senior Lecturer – Moi University School of Business & Economics
  • Lead Consultant – Various County Governments and NGOs
  • Former Director of Administration – Office of the Governor, County Government of West Pokot
  • Served on multiple task forces and advisory boards on governance reform

Ready to Transform Your Organization?

Dr. Luutsa is available for consultations, strategic workshops, and speaking engagements. Reach out to discuss how his expertise can benefit your institution.
