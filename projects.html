Highlighted Projects & Roles

Below is a selection of key roles and consulting assignments that showcase Dr. Luutsa’s expertise in governance, transformation, and institutional development.

Director of Administration – Office of the Governor, West Pokot

Oversaw institutional development, administration strategy, and operational reforms. Played a key role in shaping governance structures and citizen service delivery.

Consultant – Strategic Planning, Trans Nzoia County

Led the development of a five-year strategic plan focusing on public service efficiency, economic development, and policy implementation frameworks.

Trainer – Capacity Building for County Assemblies

Facilitated workshops on leadership, ethics, performance management, and legislative development for members of various county assemblies.

Senior Lecturer – Moi University

Teaches Public Administration and Strategic Management. Guides postgraduate research and contributes to institutional academic development.

Governance Advisor – Kenya School of Government

Contributed to curriculum development and national dialogue on devolved governance and leadership development strategies.
